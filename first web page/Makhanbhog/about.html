About Us

Welcome to MakhanBhog Dairy Milk, your local destination for the freshest and finest dairy products! Nestled in the heart of Ghosi, we pride ourselves on offering a wide array of high-quality, locally-sourced dairy goods to meet all your needs.

Our Story

Founded in 2019, MakhanBhog Dairy Milk started with a simple mission: to bring farm-fresh dairy products directly to our community. We believe in the power of local produce and the importance of supporting our local farmers. Our commitment to quality and freshness has been our guiding principle since day one.

What we do

What We Offer At MakhanBhog Dairy, we offer a diverse range of dairy products, including:

Fresh Milk: Straight from the farm, pasteurized and bottled to preserve its natural goodness.

Artisanal Cheese: A variety of handcrafted cheeses, from sharp cheddars to creamy bries.

Butter and Cream: Rich, creamy, and perfect for all your cooking and baking needs.

Yogurt and Kefir: Probiotic-rich options for a healthy and delicious snack.

Cream: Decadent, homemade ice cream in a variety of flavors.
